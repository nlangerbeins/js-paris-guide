Paris Travel Guide

Without a doubt, Paris is one of the chicest travel destinations in the wolrd. The City of Love offers the best of everything - from historic cathedrals and incomparable museums to most stylish boutiques and unbeatable cuisine. When it comes to Paris you've got covered across food, drink and culture.

This guide is based on my own experiences and includes the most delicious locations you can find in Paris.

Balagan Paris

Lovely place with great Israeli food, cool music and decor

Le Perchoir Marais

Hidden rooftop on top of the famous department store BHV MARAIS

Perruche

A green oasis where you can sit down to lunch, have a drink, admire the most beautiful sunsets and party

Le Bon Georges

The classic Paris bistro, features well-sourced meats & vegetables served in an authentic French atmosphere

LouLou

The Italian restaurant of the Musée des Arts Décoratifs is a delightful addition to the gardens of the Louvre. It's chic, cosy and a tasty treat.

Ze Kitchen Galerie

An intimate restaurant with an open kitchen at one end.

La Scène

This restaurant is a great find and an unexpected gem. The food has complex layers of flavor and the offerings of wine are exceptional.

Fitzgerald

This bar restaurant is one of the best find. Great food and drinks, extremely cosy atmosphere and super nice interior design.

Virtus

A Michelin one-star restaurant: amazing ambiance, heavenly food and excellent staff.

Cache Paris

My top recommendation for the best restuarants in Paris. 10 from 10.

Le Christine

If you're looking for a nice, intimate restaurant with modern cooking and presentation, give Le Christine a try.

Aux Crus de Bourgogne

Aux Crus de Bourgogne is a nice, traditional and authentic brasserie. The food is delicious and staff are friendly.

Les Foodies

Best restaurant in Paris! Amazing food, very good service, super ambient. Everything in the menu is worth trying and the perfect mix of flavor will let you fall in love.

Apicius

This restuarant is one of the finest restaurants in France. This is the best place if you are looking for good food.

Septime

This is fine dining without the pomp and circumstance or the arrogance. Thanks to its status as the hottest restaurant in Paris for several years now, Septime is damn near impossible to get into.

Anahi

This restaurant manages to be both intimate and sexy as well as all-inclusive and neighborly.
